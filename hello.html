Web Dev Summer Program

Even during a pandemic, it is important for students to have access and opportunities to learn STEM. Through this free, five-week online camp that I led and organized, students learned how to create a website on a social action topic of their choosing using HTML/CSS. Rather than being a normal coding camp, our program focused on minority empowerment and how technology can be applied to help solve societal issues. Along with computer science, students heard from industry guest speakers from Google, Disney, and NASA from around the nation and engaged in empowering workshops about the intersection of social action and technology. It is important that all students have access to our program and technology, so ProgramHers worked with the SBUnified school district to provide all students in our program access to Chromebooks for the duration of our program if needed.
